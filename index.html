About Us

WHO ARE WE?

The E.G.C.(Evil Genius Cooperative) is a social network for individuals with Evil Genius tendencies and for other assorted types of evil-doers.

WHAT DO WE DO?

We provide a safe space for evil geniuses to communicate and collaborate with one another. We provide a positive outlet

Want to get some feedback on your plans to take over the world? Looking to rent a secret island lair? Trying to find a reputable mad scientist? Need to become a certified evil genius?

Then the E.G.C. is here to help.

Since 2018

Discover Our History

The Evil Genius Cooperative was founded in 2018 by a person who shall only be identified as N.T.B. Her goal in forming this organization was to provide a positive outlet for individuals, much like herself, that possess evil genius tendencies but who don't want to actually take over the world for nefarious purposes. People always told her to "harness your powers for good" but no one ever told her how to do that.


Thus, the idea for the E.G.C. was born. We are a social network for evil geniuses and other like-minded individuals(minions, villains, mad scientists, masterminds, dictators, psychopaths, lunatics, et cetera). Imagine what we could accomplish if we utilized our combined skills and resources towards a common goal for the betterment of all mankind.


Members of the E.G.C. are allowed to post their "Plots for World Domination"(a.k.a. ideas for how to change the world for the better) in our Forum. Other members can comment on these postings and are able to vote for the 5 ideas that they like the best. At the end of the year, the 3 "Plots for World Domination" that have received the most votes will become the focus of that year's World Domination Summit. The World Domination Summit is our annual two-day conference where evil-doers from around the world unite to try and tackle a global issue like hunger, homelessness, or poverty.
